Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information you voluntarily provide:

  • Account information (email address, password)
  • Expense entries (amount, category, date, description)
  • Profile settings and preferences

We do not collect any financial account numbers, credit card details, or banking credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense tracking functionality:

  • To display your expense history and summaries
  • To generate category breakdowns and insights
  • To maintain your account and preferences
  • To communicate service updates (if you opt in)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database with industry-standard encryption. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with external financial services, banks, or payment processors. Your expense data remains entirely within the application. We may use third-party hosting services for data storage, which are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your stored data at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Correct or update any inaccurate information

6. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us to delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application.
